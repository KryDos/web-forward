WebFWD Partners

WebFWD partners with companies and organizations aligned with WebFWD's mission to accelerate the future of the Web for the public good.

Regional

We've locked arms with a number of location-based accelerators and entrepreneurship programs to offer expertise in open innovation.

 

  • The Awesome Foundation for the Arts and Sciences is a global alliance of micro-trustees dedicated to forwarding the interest of awesomeness in the universe. It provides monthly, no-strings-attached grants of $1,000 to support innovative projects and efforts that surprise and delight worldwide.

    Visit The Awesome Foundation

  • The Designer Fund is a community of designers who invest in designer founders through mentorship and angel funding, like AngelList for designers. Industry leading designers representing major platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Google, Twitter, LinkedIn), consultancies (IDEO, Cooper, Jump Associates, Frog, Method), and startups (Path, Flipboard, Peel) select applying candidates for angel investments and referrals to partners including programs like WebFWD.

    Visit The Designer Fund

  • Pollenizer creates web businesses. We take innovative ideas and turn them into investable businesses. Some would say we are an incubator but we think we're something altogether different. Since inception, we have created a process that gets out portfolio companies in front of customer quickly and measures performance for maximum impact and investor confidence.

    Visit Pollenizer

  • Rockstart is an Amsterdam-based technology accelerator that helps promising teams from around the world launch their startups and break through on a global scale, providing funding, office space, mentorship, coaching and trips to Silicon Valley for its teams.

    Visit Rockstart

  • Seedcamp is an early-stage micro seed investment fund and mentoring programme that was was created to jumpstart the entrepreneurial community in Europe by connecting next generation developers and entrepreneurs with over 1200 mentors. Across 10 global Mini Seedcamps spread throughout the year and our annual week-long forum (Seedcamp Week), we bring together EMEA’s top young founders with a world-class network of mentors in one place.

    Visit Seedcamp

  • Teens in Tech Labs provides tools and resources to young entrepreneurs globally to help foster entrepreneurship at a young age. Founded on the premise that entrepreneurship doesn’t happen at a certain age or a certain location, Teens in Tech Labs provides a support community and network to help young entrepreneurs create innovative and disruptive businesses.

    Visit Teens in Tech

Service Providers

We're pleased to offer our teams a great roster of products, services and experts.

 

  • AgileZen is an easy-to-use project management tool that helps people organize their work visually and collaborate with a team. More than a simple to-do list, AgileZen shows all the steps work has to go through to be complete and where everything is in the process. This means that a user can quickly see what's important to their team and what needs to be worked on next.

    Visit AgileZen

  • Since early 2006, Amazon Web Services (AWS) has provided companies of all sizes with an infrastructure web services platform in the cloud. With AWS you can requisition compute power, storage, and other services–gaining access to a suite of elastic IT infrastructure services as your business demands them.

    Visit Amazon Web Services

  • Balsamiq is the maker of Mockups and myBalsamiq, the rapid wireframing tools that reproduce the experience of sketching so that teams can focus on what's important when designing interfaces. We're a fast-growing, but small and personable company that competes on usability and service. We believe work should be fun, and that life istoo short for bad software.

    Visit Balsamiq

  • Braintree helps businesses process credit card payments by providing a merchant account, payment gateway, recurring billing and credit card storage. They're unlike others in the industry: they think and do things differently and are frequently told by their customers that they are the best payments company they've ever worked with.

    Visit Braintree

  • (mt) Media Temple is a web hosting and cloud services provider headquartered in Los Angeles, California. Since 1998, we have provided businesses and consumers around the world with professional-class service to host websites, applications, virtual servers, email, and other rich Internet content. Our infrastructure serves approximately 100,000 customers and over 1 million websites in top-tier datacenters on the east and west coasts of the United States.

    Visit Media Temple

  • Pivotal Labs is a software development consultancy working with early stage startups, growth stage startups, and small innovative groups within larger enterprises. Pivotal works with teams to co-develop products rapidly and iteratively, building a solid technical foundation and a culture that supports rapid product development over the long term.

    Visit Pivotal Labs

  • Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati is the premier legal advisor to technology and growth enterprises worldwide, as well as the investment banks and venture capital firms that finance them. The firm has been privileged to help lay the groundwork for some of the world's most innovative technology, life sciences, and growth companies. As the nation's leading law firm representing venture-backed companies, we have counseled thousands of entrepreneurs starting new businesses, advising on the formation of companies, equity structures, negotiations with investors, intellectual property rights, and other issues vital to their success.

    Visit Wilson Sonsini Goodrich & Rosati
