About WebFWD by Mozilla

WebFWD ("Web Forward") is Mozilla's Open Innovation program. We support Open Source projects which extend the Web — providing participants with mentorship from industry experts, access to the Mozilla global network, as well as infrastructure and other world-class resources.

WebFWD gives us a chance to help builders and entrepreneurs worldwide to create open solutions that push the Web forward, empowering users with choice and control of their online lives.

We're looking for:

  • Projects and companies that align with the principles for the Web as outlined in Mozilla's Manifesto, which empowers users to control their presence on the Web and become creators as well as consumers of Web applications and content - and provide a valuable social benefit.
  • Technologies that are orieneted towards the social and mobile Web as opposed to the desktop and OS paradigms.
  • Prototypes with working code and working (or close to working) minimum viable products.
  • Teams comprised not of hobbyists, but rather deeply committed to getting projects built, released and sustained.

Qualifying projects around the world receive:

  • A minimum six-month engagement with WebFWD
  • Regular 1:1 contact with Mozillians and access to our worldwide network of mentors, partners and scouts
  • Infrastructure support
  • Coaching through mentorship, curriculum and hands-on hackdays, meetups and events for the emerging WebFWD community
