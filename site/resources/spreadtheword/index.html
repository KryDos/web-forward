Spread the Word

As a global program, to spread awareness we rely largely on our Scouts, Mentors, Partners... and you! Below are some ways you can join us in getting the word out about how we are working to move the open Web forward!

  • Join Our Affiliates Program

    Display our logo on your website and join thousands of others as a Mozilla affiliate.

  • Use Our Logo

    Do you love what we're doing? Display our logo in your profile photo or whenever you want to share a little more about us in a non-HTML way.

  • Give a Lightening Talk

    Be our voice and present a lightening talk on WebFWD with this deck. If you really get into this, check out our Scouts program. You can hear Diane give this here.
