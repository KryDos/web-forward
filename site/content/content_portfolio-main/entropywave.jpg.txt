Info: Entropy Wave is a San Francisco based company specializing in open video technology. We provide complete systems for video encoding and distribution to content producers and distributors, as well as an open source, Gstreamer-based software media platform for application and embedded developers. Entropy Wave products enable customers benefit from the freedom and cost-effectiveness of open source and open video technology.
